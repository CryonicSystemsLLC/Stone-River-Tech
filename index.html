Why Stone River Tech

We install it and configure it.

01

IT Background. Not Just a Cable Puller.

Most low-voltage contractors can mount a camera and crimp a connector. We configure the managed switch, segment the VLANs, set up the NVR for secure remote access, and troubleshoot network issues that other installers can't diagnose. You hire one company instead of two.

02

No Subscriptions. No Monthly Fees.

We deploy DW Spectrum IPVMS — a one-time perpetual license with no annual renewal. Your system is yours. No recurring cloud fees, no vendor lock-in, no price increases. Full remote access included through DW Cloud at no extra charge.

03

NDAA Compliant Hardware

Every camera and recorder we install meets NDAA Section 889 requirements. Safe for government buildings, schools, contractors with federal work, and any facility that takes cybersecurity seriously. No banned components. No compromised firmware.

04

Clean Work. Labeled. Documented.

Every cable labeled at both ends. Every port documented. Every system handed over with credentials, network diagrams, and a client walkthrough. When you need service in two years, we can identify everything instantly — and so can you.
